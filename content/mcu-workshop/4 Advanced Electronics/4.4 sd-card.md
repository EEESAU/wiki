Storing data on a microSD card using Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI).
# Components
- 1x microSD card module
- 1x microSD card

---
# Instructions
With the arrow on the silkscreen facing up, the pins from top to bottom are:
- 3.3v (VCC)
- Chip Select (CS)
- Data In (DI)
- Clock (CLK)
- Data Out (DO)
- Ground (GND)
